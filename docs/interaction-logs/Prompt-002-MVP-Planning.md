## Human Input

The human redirected the build toward LegacyOS Lite as a hackathon sprint and prioritized a working product, clear problem solved, impressive demonstration, strong AI functionality, and strong visual presentation.

## MVP Scope

The selected user journey was:

1. Select a role.
2. Complete an AI interview.
3. Generate summary, timeline, and knowledge profile.
4. Build a knowledge graph.
5. Calculate a knowledge risk score.
6. Ask questions through AI search.

## AI Assistance

The AI translated the MVP into an incremental seven-day build plan, then implemented the app across backend, frontend, local database, graph visualization, search, testing, and demo readiness.

## Human Review

The human repeatedly selected stability and demo usefulness over broader completeness, especially around Day 2 interview reliability, repository ingestion, and Day 7 testing.
