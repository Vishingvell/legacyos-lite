## Human Input

The human identified a knowledge continuity problem: important role knowledge is often trapped in individual employees, meeting notes, audit notes, incident notes, and informal handover context.

## Development Need

The hackathon solution needed to show that a lightweight system could capture role knowledge, preserve operational context, and make it searchable before a team loses access to the person or context.

## AI Assistance

The AI helped turn the problem into a focused demo scope with six core capabilities:

- AI interview engine
- Knowledge repository
- Knowledge timeline
- Knowledge graph visualization
- Knowledge risk score
- AI knowledge search

## Human Review

The human confirmed that the demo should solve a clear practical problem and avoid overbuilding beyond the hackathon MVP.
