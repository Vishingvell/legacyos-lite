## Human Input

The human wanted the product to identify knowledge risk, not only store notes.

## Risk Score Decisions

The MVP risk score considers signals such as:

- Critical operations role
- Missing or unclear documentation
- Single fallback person
- Incident or audit phrases
- Unclear process ownership
- Uneven extraction confidence

## AI Assistance

The AI implemented risk scoring, risk drivers, dashboard risk meter, and search answers that explain continuity risks.

## Human Review

The human tested incident scenarios and reviewed whether risk answers were useful, relevant, and grounded in the selected role and repository evidence.
