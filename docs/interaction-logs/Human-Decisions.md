The following decisions were made by the human during the LegacyOS Lite hackathon build:

- Build LegacyOS Lite as the hackathon-focused product.
- Prioritize working demo impact over completeness.
- Focus on roles that matter in organizational continuity.
- Treat the interview system as the core data input for the whole product.
- Add meeting notes, audit notes, and handover notes inside the product.
- Keep profiles role-specific and clickable from the dashboard.
- Make search evidence inspectable and removable.
- Avoid irrelevant cross-role search evidence.
- Preserve local persistence for reliable demos.
- Treat Vercel storage as temporary unless persistent storage is configured.
- Remove internal prompt archives and non-MVP material from the public repository.
- Publish the repository for hackathon review with ownership notices in place.
