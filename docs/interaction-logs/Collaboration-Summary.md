## Summary

LegacyOS Lite was built through human-led product direction and AI-assisted implementation.

The human identified the problem, selected the hackathon scope, reviewed the product behavior, tested the deployed app, and made final decisions about what should remain in the public repository.

The AI assisted by brainstorming implementation options, writing code, debugging deployment issues, generating test cases, improving UI behavior, and preparing public review materials.

## Evidence of Human-AI Iteration

- The human moved the project from broad planning to a focused hackathon MVP.
- The human prioritized interview stability before moving into later features.
- The human identified repository notes as necessary input alongside interviews.
- The human reviewed graph/search screenshots and flagged irrelevant or overwhelming results.
- The human requested role-specific dashboard access after noticing a profile appeared missing.
- The human requested repository cleanup before public release.
- The AI implemented each selected direction and verified behavior with local and live tests.

## Final Public Scope

This repository contains only the LegacyOS Lite hackathon MVP materials needed to review the working demo and development process.
